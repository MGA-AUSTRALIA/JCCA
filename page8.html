1974 Jensen Healey Manual

$20,000 . 00$12
his 1974 Jensen Healey Sports Mark II left the Kelvin Way factory on 15 November 1974 and was originally delivered to Los Angeles California.
White with black trim the car was then shipped to Australia and I purchased it from its Barossa Valley owner in 2012.
This is my second Jensen Healey, the first was in my University years - that car had twin Dellorto carburettors.
This car, with its Lotus Twin Cam 2.0 litre engine (0-60mph in 6 secs according to factory specs) has twin Stromberg carburettors and the difference is life changing!! - the Stromberg's have been rebuilt (all jets etc replaced) and the car purrs!
You do not buy a Jensen Healey for speed. You buy the car for the joy of hearing the engine and enjoying the scenery and fresh air as you cruise the open country road.
This is easily an everyday drive car.
I believe this particular car would have to be the best example of its kind in Western Australia, if not the country.
It has been lovingly cared for.
The 4 speed gearbox has been converted to 5 speed - it now loves cruising the streets of the city just as much as stretching its legs on those winding country roads.
