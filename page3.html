Become a Member of the Jensen Car Club of Australia

Welcome to the official website of the Jensen Car Club of Australia.
The club was formed in 1981 by a group of eager owner enthusiasts in Melbourne – and quickly spread to include chapters in all states throughout Australia.


Today the club has around 200 members including many international owners who appreciate the club mainly via our top-quality quarterly magazine “The Interceptor”.
The club has an active social calendar in all States – attending Car shows to display our vehicles and getting together for club events.  The Jensen Car Club of Australia does not hold a concours event.
Our annual Club Rally is held in rotation around capital cities during the Easter long weekend every year.
At our annual rally a “Show ‘n Shine” display is held and members vote for “The People’s Choice Award”. The definition of this award is – you are voting for the vehicle you would most like to take home with you!
We are a friendly club with simple principles of sharing knowledge, friendship and camaraderie centered around the Jensen Marque, with vehicles produced between 1936 and 1976.
