Your continued club membership is very important to us, and we look forward to welcoming you back for another successful year. This year official membership cards will be issued for the first time. Please complete and return this signed form to the address below – irrespective of the payment method options listed. If paying via electronic means – please use your Surname as a reference for identification purposes. Note that your membership does not become valid or membership card sent - until this signed form is also received by the Membership Secretary, via email or post
