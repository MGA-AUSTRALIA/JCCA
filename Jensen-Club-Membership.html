Our Mission

The purposes of the association are— To promote the preservation, use and restoration of Jensen motor vehicles. To establish a register of members’ vehicles including but not limited to photographs, ownership details, history, registration particulars, vehicle particulars and modifications. To provide members with advice and support on mechanical and technical issues, the sourcing of spare parts and the maintenance and repair of vehicles. To generate on a regular basis information of relevance to members including but not limited to activities involving members, summaries of meetings, general news of interest to members and liaison with counterpart organizations within Australia and abroad. To arrange and facilitate club runs and social events. To disseminate information via any means considered appropriate including but not limited to print publications, electronic publications, social media and internet-based sites or forums.

Annual subscription

Annual subscription and fee on joining
(1) At each annual general meeting, the Association must determine the amount of the annual subscription (if any) for the following financial year.
(2) The date for payment of the annual subscription is 1 January.
(3) A member whose subscription (if any) has not been received by 31 March shall cease to be a member.
(4) The Association may determine that a lower subscription or no subscription is payable by associate members, life members or temporary members.
(5) The Association may determine that any new member who joins after the start of a financial year must, for that financial year, pay a fee equal to—
. (a) the full annual subscription; or (b) a pro rata annual subscription based on the remaining part of the financial year; or
. (c) a fixed amount determined from time to time by the Association.
(6) The rights of a member (including the right to vote) who has not paid the annual subscription by the due date, unless payment is exempted under rule 12(4), are suspended until the subscription is paid.

Privacy Statement

How we collect your personal information. The Jensen Car Club of Australia (JCCA) collects information from the membership application form and other information provided by the member to the club. It is our aim to protect our member’s information and to provide a safe and secure on-line experience. Our website is used to gather information about our members, enabling us to provide a secure area of interaction between current financial club members. Club member’s information is only available to club officials for the purpose of administering the club. How we use and keep your personal information. We collect your contact information (name, address, e-mail address and phone numbers etc.) to process your club membership and organise club activities.
Privacy
The information will be kept confidential to the club and at no time will this information be traded, swapped, exchanged or sold to any other third party or company. The information is held on a secure third party website contracted to JCCA. Individual members information is available to committee members and club officers authorised by committee for the purpose of administering club activities. Club members can access and update their details by logging into the JCCA website, downloading the form and emailing back to the club.
Privacy
The JCCA Inc’s website does not handle ANY financial transactions conducted in relation to membership transacted Transactions are conducted via email.
