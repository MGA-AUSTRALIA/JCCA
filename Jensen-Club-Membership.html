Membership FAQ

Membership Benefits 

Membership conveys the following benefits:
• Participation in club events and the annual rally
• Quarterly magazine, ‘The Interceptor’
• Concessional registration (in some States)
• Technical support • Facebook (closed group)
• Regalia

Immediate family members are automatically included as club members. 

Associate membership is open to persons aged under 16 years who join in their own right (membership fees waived). 

Our Mission

 We are here to
• promote the preservation, use and restoration of Jensen motor vehicles
• facilitate club runs and social events
• provide members with advice and support on mechanical and technical issues
• establish a register of members’ vehicles
• disseminate information of relevance to members
• liaise with counterpart organizations within Australia and abroad

Annual subscription

The date for payment of the annual subscription (adult membership) is 1 January. The subscription may be varied but is currently $80 (adult membership) and free for associate (junior) members. Payment of the annual subscription gives members the right to vote and speak at club meetings and is a requirement for participation in concessional registration schemes.  

Privacy Statement

The club adheres to the privacy principles set out in Victorian and Federal law as applicable to Not For Profit organizations. The club collects information about members via the membership application form and any other information provided by members to the club. Membership information is used by the club’s officials for the purpose of administering the club and organizing club activities. Under Victorian law and the club constitution, information contained in the membership register is available to all members on request.
