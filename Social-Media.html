To day the club has around 200 members including many international owners who appreciate the club mainly via our top-quality quarterly magazine 'The Interceptor'. The club has an active social calendar in all States – attending Car shows to display our vehicles and getting together for club events. The Jensen Car Club of Australia does not hold a concours event. Our annual Club Rally is held in rotation around capital cities during the Easter long weekend every year. At our annual rally a 'Show ‘n Shine' display is held and members vote for 'The People’s Choice Award'. The definition of this award is – you are voting for the vehicle you would most like to take home with you!
We are a friendly club with simple principles of sharing knowledge, friendship and camaraderie centered around the Jensen marque.


The name of the incorporated association is “Jensen Car Club Incorporated” conducting business as “The Jensen Car Club of Australia”.

About the Jensen Car Club of Australia

Welcome to the official website of the Jensen Car Club of Australia. 
The club was formed in 1981 by a group of eager owner enthusiasts in Melbourne – and quickly spread to include chapters in all states throughout Australia.
